Recipe for Turmeric Rice

Home

Description

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Turmeric

Steps

  1. Clean the rice by filling it in a pan with water, then pouring out the water
  2. Fil the rice with water again
  3. Start heating up the rice on the stove
  4. Add the turmeric and mix it with the water
  5. Wait for water to start boiling, then turn off the stove
  6. Move pan away from stove once the rice absorbs the water
