Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti noodles in a large pot of salted boiling water until they are al dente, then drain them.
  2. Sauté the diced onion and garlic in olive oil, then add the ground beef and cook until it is fully browned.
  3. Season the meat, pour in the jar of marinara sauce, and let it simmer on low heat for 15 minutes.
  4. Toss the drained pasta directly into the sauce or layer the sauce over individual plates of noodles and garnish with Parmesan.
